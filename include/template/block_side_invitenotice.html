What does it mean by "different state"?

  • Yet to buy: You've invited your friend to get registered, but she/he hasn't bought any deals.
  • Rebated: congratulations, you've got your rebate
  • To be rebated: your friend has bought a deal, and you'll get the rebate in 24 hours, please wait.
  • Rejected: invitation is invalid because the mobile number has already been registered or other reasons.
  • Expired: your friend hasn't bought a deal within 7 days, and your invitation expired.

Can I get rebate by inviting myself?

No. We would examine the invitation carefully. If you cheat in inviting, your invitation would be rejected and you won't get rebate.
